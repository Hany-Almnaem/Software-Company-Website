    Our Products

    Management of legal offices

    An integrated electronic system for managing cases and contracts in the legal office, starting from: - Contract management: digital archiving of documents;

    Follow-up of renewals. - Case management: archiving all cases in which the company is involved, identifying the plaintiff or defendant party, following up on the required minutes, papers, and expenses related to each case, along with its value and date of payment, and issuing a report thereon - Jurisprudence section: the possibility of adding the text of the jurisprudence with a full explanation of the decision. The possibility of linking the text of the jurisprudence with the case number and the case for which the jurisprudence was issued, and the ability to search for any text within multiple options (text number, text date, case number ....) This system is the basis for all legal offices to manage their legal contracts and the cases they deal with, as the program provides many important statistics and reports in an optimal manner, which saves a lot of time and effort.

    Enterprise resource Management

    An integrated electronic system for managing accounting and warehouse operations in the company,

    starting from: Managing financial accounts: organizing the accounts guide, financial entries, financial movement, account card, trial balance, cash flow operations, budget reports, and income statement. Warehouse Operations Management: Organizing entry, exit, and transfer of materials, managing daily operations, loading import operations with purchase expenses, loading selling operations with transportation and loading expenses, issuing daily movement reports of materials within warehouses, calculating the weighted cost of outputs, and issuing inventory lists for warehouses and materials Manufacturing operations management: Organizing manufacturing operations, extracting raw materials and production, adding direct costs to production operations and distributing them to reach the direct cost of the product. This system is considered the basis for all institutions to manage their financial and warehouse operations, and it relies on international foundations in accounting and warehouses. All programs issue the financial entry that this system receives and distributes to the accounts of each operation. It is considered responsible for all financial, warehouse, statistical and administrative reports that the institution needs in managing own resources.

    Treasury and cash Management

    An integrated electronic system for archiving the receipt and payment operations of the fund or treasury department in the company,

    starting from: - An integrated archive of received arrest warrants with all information related to them, including number and date; With the possibility of dealing with more than one fund and bank - An integrated archive of payment and payment orders with all information related to them, including number, date and beneficiary; With the possibility of classifying and paying it from any fund or bank with the possibility of archiving and printing checks. - An integrated archive of exchange orders with the possibility of calculating taxes and fees on exchanges, identifying the sections of exchanges, verifying the existence of the available budget for the specific exchange process, and showing the required budget balance - Integrated reports on the daily movement of the treasury according to date, including arrest, payment and disbursement operations, with integrated reports for each fund, advance, expense section, budget, fund and bank inventory report, budget and advance balance report This system helps the management of cash and banking operations to manage payments and receipts, cash inventory operations, monitor bank balances and archive documents related to this process without any delay and without being linked to the financial and accounting departments, and thus monitor the cash and bank balance in a real-time and direct manner in terms of providing information and the possibility of monitoring it with the financial departments and follow-up Disbursement operations and budgets, which prevents the specified budgets from being exceeded directly.
